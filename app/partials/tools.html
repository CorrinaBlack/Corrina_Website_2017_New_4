Windows Phone dev tools for creating

Windows Phone 7 apps were created with either XNA or
Silverlight, and there were robust dev tools to support
rapid application development in both Visual Studio
and Expression Blend.


My role was Senior Research and Design Lead. My
team and I defined the target developer persona, and
we designed rapid application development tools in
both Visual Studio and Expression Blend including
the phone emulator, aspects of the design time
experience, project templates, and profiling tools. My
core responsibilities included research, interaction
design, visual design, motion design, prototyping, and
art direction.
