Microsoft IoT for
a connected world

Microsoft IoT provides an ever growing suite of solutions
from Windows IoT core to advanced data analytics in an
effort to meet the needs of consumers and businesses
in a device, software, and service connected world.


My role was Principal Design Lead. My team and
I led research to understand the competitive
landscape and user motivations. We also
collaborated with engineering to identify
market opportunities and to define a Ux vision
demonstrating the future value of IoT for both
consumers and businesses. My core responsibilities
included user research, market analysis, Ux vision
definition, storytelling, prototyping, and art direction.


What follows are a few examples from the work my team
and I did in this space...

Research & Scenario Exploration

Design direction/lead for competitive understanding, user research, and scenario exploration

Problem & Opportunity Analysis

Design direction/lead for problem and opportunity analysis, review, and iteration

Vision & Storytelling

Design direction/lead for Ux vision and storytelling to explore the future value of IoT
