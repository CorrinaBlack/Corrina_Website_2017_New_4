Windows Phone 8.1
for people power

Windows Phone 8.1 was the fourth release of a new Windows
Phone operating system with an innovative design language,
targeting capacitive touch devices, and ushering Microsoft
into the mobile first world.


My role was Principal Design Lead. My team and I designed the
phone's first-party app experiences including camera, photo
hub, calendar, email, music & entertainment, games, internet
explorer, and phone companion apps for Windows. We
designed or consulted on key third-party app experiences
as well. We also designed a framework for custom lock
screen experiences. My core responsibilities included
interaction design, visual design, motion design,
prototyping, and art direction.
