Silverlight extends the platform's reach

In 2010, Silverlight extended it's reach into the embedded
devices space. This was an area where Microsoft had a
strong foothold with Windows CE, and the intent was to
build off the Silverlight RIA platform momentum and
enable rapid application development for the creation
of rich and immersive embedded device experiences.


My role was Senior Research and Design Lead. Much
of the developer experience leveraged the work my
team and I designed for the Silverlight RIA platform.
We also designed and helped build sample apps to
both inspire and demonstrate the capabilities of the
platform. My core responsibilities included research,
interaction design, visual design, motion design,
prototyping, and art direction.
